About Capulse

Research for people who want to understand, not a tip.

Built for retail traders and investors who want serious analysis without opening a new trading relationship to get it.

Most retail research in India arrives as a call: buy this, sell that, target here. It asks you to trust a stranger’s conviction instead of building your own. Capulse takes the opposite approach — it shows the fundamentals, momentum, valuation and risk behind a number, and lets you draw the conclusion.

We built Capulse as a chat-first research assistant on purpose. No ticker tape, no dashboard to learn — just a place to ask a real question about a stock, an options level, or your own portfolio, and get a straight, sourced answer back.

Every score and probability Capulse produces stands behind a SEBI-registered Research Analyst, the same accountability structure used by traditional research houses, applied to a conversational product instead of a static PDF report.

Capulse is built by the same team behind TargetCapital. Where TargetCapital is a broker-connected execution platform, Capulse is deliberately independent of it — a research tool for anyone, whether or not they ever open an account anywhere else.
