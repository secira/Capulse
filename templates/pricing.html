FAQ

Questions before you upgrade

Do I need to connect a broker to use Target Capital?

No. The Starter plan works with a manually uploaded portfolio. Connecting a broker (from Growth onward) unlocks live execution and real-time portfolio sync.

Which brokers are supported?

Dhan, Zerodha, Angel One, Upstox, Fyers, Shoonya (Finvasia), Alice Blue and 5paisa — with more added as we validate integrations.

Is this investment advice?

No. Signals and scores are educational and informational. Target Capital is not a substitute for a SEBI-registered investment adviser — we show you the reasoning, the decision is yours.

Can I cancel anytime?

Yes — every paid plan is month-to-month with no lock-in. Downgrade to Starter at any time and keep read-only access to your history.
