How it works

One chat window. Four things it’s actually doing.

Capulse looks like a chat. Underneath, every answer runs through the same disciplined pipeline — here’s what happens between your question and the reply.

01

Ask anything market-related

A stock’s i-Score, an F&O signal, a concept you don’t understand, or a question about your own portfolio. Capulse classifies the question and routes it to the right engine underneath — you never see the plumbing.

02

The i-Score engine scores what you asked about

For a stock, Capulse builds a composite 0–100 i-Score from five sub-scores — fundamentals, momentum, valuation, sentiment and risk — each calculated from live NSE/BSE data, quarterly filings and news sentiment. You see the sub-scores, not just the headline number.

03

The F&O engine generates ranked signals, not tips

Ask about NIFTY or BANKNIFTY and Capulse runs a 3-layer analysis — time filter, direction engine (VWAP + Supertrend + DMI), and momentum confirmation (ADX + ATR + OI) — to generate ranked trade setups with entry, SL, and target. Confidence-scored 0–100. You set your own risk before acting.

04

A Research Analyst stands behind it

Every scoring methodology is pre-approved by our SEBI-registered Research Analyst. High-conviction scores and signal outputs are sampled and reviewed on an ongoing basis, with a named analyst of record and a full audit trail.

05

It remembers, on the paid plan

Your risk appetite, the sectors you keep asking about, your uploaded portfolio, and the stop-loss/target levels you’ve logged — Capulse holds this across sessions on Capulse Plus. You can view or clear it whenever you like.

No broker connection

Portfolio analysis works from manually added holdings — Capulse never connects to a live brokerage account or places an order.
