Platform Features

Everything your clients need. Nothing they don't.

A complete AI-powered trading support suite built for the Indian market — delivered under your brand.

Portfolio Hub

Unified multi-broker dashboard across 11 asset classes — equities, F&O, MF, bonds, crypto, real estate and more. Real-time PnL and analytics.

AI Research Co-Pilot

RAG-powered research assistant with real-time web search (Perplexity), stock deep-dives, and AI-generated trade suggestions with cited sources.

Live Market Pulse

Real-time Nifty 50 heatmap, sector performance, top movers, index strip, and an interactive AI query interface with voice support.

Scentric Risk Engine

Portfolio Pulse health score, Risk Heat Map per asset class, Goal-Based Monitoring, and behavioural guardrails that warn against risk-profile violations.

I-Score AI Engine

7-factor weighted score per stock — qualitative, quantitative, sentiment, trend — powered by Claude AI and Perplexity real-time search.

Trading Signals

Transparent, experience-gated AI signals with entry, target and stop-loss. Supports equities, F&O, ETFs and commodities with plan-based access control.
