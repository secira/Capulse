How partners onboard
  1. Broker emails partners@targetcapital.in with their use case and webhook URL.
  2. You click New Partner above and fill in their details.
  3. The platform shows the API key once — you copy it and email it to them securely.
  4. They authenticate every request with Authorization: Bearer <key>.
  5. They subscribe to symbols/indices via POST /api/partner/v1/subscriptions.
  6. Whenever a qualifying signal fires, our server POSTs it to their webhook with an HMAC signature.
