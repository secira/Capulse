Dispute Resolution

If a complaint is not resolved to your satisfaction, you may escalate it through the investor grievance mechanisms established by the Securities and Exchange Board of India (SEBI):

  • SEBI SCORES — file your complaint online at scores.sebi.gov.in.
  • SMART ODR (Online Dispute Resolution) — SEBI's neutral mediation & arbitration portal at smartodr.in.
  • Exchange IGRC — Investor Grievance Resolution Committees at NSE and BSE.

Target Capital is a technology research and analytics platform. Trade execution is carried out by your connected SEBI-registered stock broker; broker-related disputes are governed by that broker's terms and the relevant exchange's bye-laws.
